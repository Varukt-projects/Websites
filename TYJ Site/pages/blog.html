Best Time to Visit Himachal

Snow (Dec–Mar) for skiing and winter vibes; greenery (Apr–Jun) for cafes and hikes; autumn (Sep–Oct) for clear skies.

How to Plan Rann Utsav

Book tents early for Nov–Feb. Visit sunset point, local crafts, and nearby villages. Consider premium tent city upgrades.

Rishikesh Rafting Safety Tips

Always choose licensed operators, wear PFDs, follow captain instructions, and check fitness requirements.
