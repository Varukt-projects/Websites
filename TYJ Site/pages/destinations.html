Destinations

Himachal Pradesh

Manali, Tirthan Valley, Shimla–Kufri, Kasol.

Best time: Dec–Mar (snow), Apr–Jun (pleasant), Sep–Oct (clear).

How to reach: Fly to Kullu/Chandigarh + road transfers.

Uttarakhand

Rishikesh, Auli, Valley of Flowers, Nainital.

Best time: Oct–Jun; Jan–Mar for skiing in Auli.

How to reach: Fly to Dehradun; trains to Haridwar/Rishikesh.

Rajasthan

Jaisalmer, Udaipur, Jaipur, Jodhpur.

Best time: Oct–Mar (cooler desert & cities).

How to reach: Major airports: Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur; trains and roads well-connected.

Gujarat

Rann of Kutch, Gir, Dwarka–Somnath.

Best time: Nov–Feb (Rann Utsav), Dec–Apr for Gir.

How to reach: Airports: Bhuj, Rajkot, Jamnagar; roads excellent.
